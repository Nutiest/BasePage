My Three Favorite Parts about Being 16

Number One

In the days coming up to my birthday I seem to be happier and happier. I become more excited, and I can't wait until I can drive. I'll have to wait another week until I can test for my Drivers License, but thats ok. I don't have many super important places to go to by myself anyway.

Number Two

There's a sense of maturity that comes with being 16 that feels so great. There's just so much freedom and responsibility that I've been oh so waiting for. When I turn 16 I'm honestly just gonna drive aroudn for fun.

Number Three

My birthday is on Valentine's Day so it's funny to see people freak out when they realize when my birthday is. I'm a quiet person in general, so typically people have to find out through other people blurting it out the day before my birthday. And since my birthday is on Valentine's Day I get leeway with not really having to get my valentine a gift (Even though I do anyway).
